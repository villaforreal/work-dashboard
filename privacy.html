Privacy Policy for Work Dashboard

Effective Date: March 18, 2026

1. Introduction

Work Dashboard ("we," "our," or "the App") is a client-side productivity tool designed to help users manage focus sprints and view their daily schedule. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your data when you integrate your Google Calendar with the App.

2. Data Access and Usage

If you choose to connect your Google Calendar, Work Dashboard requests access to the strictly read-only scope: https://www.googleapis.com/auth/calendar.readonly.

We use this access exclusively to retrieve your event titles and start times for the current day to display them on your personal dashboard and trigger local, browser-based notifications prior to meetings. The App cannot create, edit, or delete your calendar events.

3. Data Storage and Security

Work Dashboard is a completely serverless, client-side web application.

  • We do not own, operate, or maintain any backend databases or servers.
  • Your Google OAuth access tokens are stored strictly locally on your device within your browser's localStorage.
  • Your calendar data is fetched directly from Google's servers to your device's browser. It is never transmitted to, routed through, or stored on any external server belonging to the developer or any third party.

4. Data Sharing and Disclosure

Because we do not collect or store your data on any external servers, we do not share, sell, or transfer your Google user data to any third parties, advertising networks, or analytics platforms.

5. Google API Services User Data Policy Compliance

Work Dashboard's use and transfer to any other app of information received from Google APIs will adhere to the Google API Services User Data Policy, including the Limited Use requirements.

6. Your Rights and Revoking Access

You can revoke Work Dashboard's access to your Google Calendar at any time by navigating to your Google Account's Security settings (https://myaccount.google.com/permissions) and removing access for "Work Dashboard". Clicking "Disconnect" within the App will also clear your local browser session.
